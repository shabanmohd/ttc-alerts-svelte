## Badge Style Legend

- **TTC Red**: Regular service buses - Red background (#C8102E) with white text
- **White with Red Border**: Limited service buses (10, 99, 115, 119, 160, 162, 166, 167, 169) - White background with red border and text (#C8102E)
- **White with Gray Border**: Community service buses (400, 402, 403, 404, 405, 406) - White background with gray border and text (#6B7280)
- **White with Blue Border**: Night service buses (300-series) - White background with blue border and text (#003DA5)
- **TTC Express Green**: Express service buses (900-series) - Green background (#00853F) with white text

## Notes

- Limited service routes: 10, 99, 115, 119, 160, 162, 166, 167, 169
- All bus routes use partial route matching in the alert categorization system
- Route numbers are matched as strings to handle potential alpha suffixes (e.g., "71B")
